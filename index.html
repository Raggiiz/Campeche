Know our brand

A cigarette with a biodegradable filter that has a tree or plant seed (being specified the type of seed in the pack). In addition to the biodegradable filter, its tissue paper and Tobacco products are organic and made in a sustainable way and seeking the greatest harm reduction possible to the consumer. Presenting cigarettes with nicotine differences, a pack will have bigger amount of nicotine, while other packs will have less, the smoker can thus go reducing your nicotine doses, a way to help reduce your consumption or even stop.

SUSTAINABLE ECOLOGICAL FACTOR

Campeche Bio Solutions has an immense concern for our ecosystem, thinking about it, a way was developed to help it through a simple and everyday act like smoking a cigarette. A bad habit, however extremely frequent, is that smokers throw their butts anywhere after finishing their cigarette. A normal cigarette butt takes one to two years to decompose, causing numerous problems for the environment. Our proposal is that of a biodegradable butt and with a huge differential, a seed placed in each filter, after finishing your cigarette and throwing it on the ground, the process of biodegradation of the butt starts, and with that the seed in your filter will begin to germinate and then the growth of the plant. With this initiative, the possibilities are immense, changing a scenario in which a butt in the forest would be degrading to something positive, taking into account that vegetation would be born in this place.
